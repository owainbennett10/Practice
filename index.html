Welcome to Gwendraeth Valley Tarmacadam

With over 35 years' experience in the trade, Gwendraeth Valley Tarmacadam are a well-established provider of tarmacadam and resurfacing services to both domestic and commercial clients. From private driveways and paths to public road resurfacing and maintenance, we undertake all types of tarmacing work. We can also hire out paver and roller machinery.

Local authority approved

We are local authority approved contractors, and certified to supply and lay thin layers of asphalt and tarmacadam.

24/7 services

We are open 24/7, which means you can rely on us to respond to your queries or serve you at any time of the day or night. Do not hesitate to call us to inquire about our services.

Why choose us?

We have many benefits of why you should chose us?. As we have fully trained and qualified staff, Constructionline registered, Road Haulage Association member and Environment Agency approved.
